How to Remove Stains from an Iron Dish

  • Mix equal parts of baking soda and water to create a paste.
  • Apply the paste to the stained areas of the iron dish.
  • Let it sit for 15-30 minutes to allow the paste to work on the stains.
  • Gently scrub the stains using a non-abrasive sponge or cloth.
  • Rinse the dish thoroughly with water.
  • Once the stains are removed, wash the iron dish with warm, soapy water and dry it thoroughly.
